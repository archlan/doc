ArchLan

Arch-based distribution designed for fun and minimalism.

Reliable

ArchLan Linux is a minimal and robust distribution using i3wm to make it able to run on most machines, even the least powerful.

Appealing

I tried to make looks based on glassmorphism using blur effects on certain applications, bright colors and good-looking wallpapers.

Powerful

With OEM support for the AUR, ArchLan allows you to do whatever you want to do on your machine, using the power of Linux.
